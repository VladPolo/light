About

Honeycomb touch light with RGB remote control is a unique geometric minimalist design that will decorate your home. Can be placed in the living room, bedroom, study, restaurant, hotel, etc. The hexagonal shape allows the modules to be connected in various designs. The lamp turns on and off by touch, each module turns on separately.

Each module can be used separately or create a whole composition to your taste. You can hang this lamp in your bedroom, living room, office or even your bathroom. The wall lamp takes up less space than a table lamp or a floor lamp, so it will easily fit into any interior, even in limited space in the room.
